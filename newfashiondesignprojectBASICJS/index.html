Fashion Tips & Guides

Expert advice to elevate your style

Versatility is key when styling tops for different occasions:

  • Casual Outings: Pair a crop top or halter top with high-waisted jeans and sneakers for a relaxed look.
  • Office Wear: Opt for a button-down shirt or a blouse with tailored pants or a pencil skirt.
  • Evening Events: Choose tops with embellishments like rhinestones or sequins, paired with sleek pants or a skirt.

Dresses that flatter your unique shape can boost your confidence:

  • Hourglass: Wrap dresses and fit-and-flare styles accentuate your natural curves.
  • Pear-shaped: A-line dresses balance proportions by highlighting the waist and flowing over the hips.
  • Apple-shaped: Empire waist dresses draw attention to the bust and flow away from the midsection.
